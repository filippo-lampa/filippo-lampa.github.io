Research

My research investigates learning and decision-making processes in complex and dynamic environments. I am interested in how artificial agents adapt over time when operating under limited information, computational constraints, and evolving objectives.

A central focus of my work is cooperative and multi-agent learning, with the goal of understanding how coordination and information sharing can improve collective performance. I study these problems through adaptive and online learning methods, with particular attention to reliability and robustness in security-critical systems such as blockchain platforms.

Research Interests

  • Adaptive and online learning
  • Cooperative and multi-agent AI
  • Sequential decision-making
  • Blockchain security and reliability
