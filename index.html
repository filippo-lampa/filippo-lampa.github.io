Research

My research studies learning and decision-making in complex, evolving environments. I am interested in how artificial agents adapt their behavior over time when faced with limited information, computational constraints, and changing objectives.

A central theme of my work is cooperative and multi-agent learning, with an emphasis on coordination, information sharing, and collective performance. I approach these problems through adaptive and online learning methods, with applications to security-critical systems such as blockchain platforms.

Research Interests

  • Adaptive and online learning
  • Cooperative and multi-agent AI
  • Sequential decision-making
  • Blockchain security and reliability
