Research

My research focuses on adaptive learning agents for sequential decision-making under resource constraints. I study online and distributed systems that balance exploration, coordination, and efficiency, with current applications to blockchain security, smart contract testing, and vulnerability analysis.

The broader goal is to design learning systems that remain reliable in dynamic environments while producing insights transferable to general multi-agent settings.

Interests

  • Adaptive and online learning
  • Multi-agent systems
  • Blockchain security
  • LLM-based testing and analysis
